BlueJscript is not

A complete browser system: Most of BlueJcript examples have been written in standard HTML. If you want the browser the internet use you preferred browser

A tutorial on Webservices: You will realize that you can drive BlueJscript trough webservices. There will be examples on how to do it but you will need to do your homework on undersanding the technology
